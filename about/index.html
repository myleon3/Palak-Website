I am a Fashion Photographer based out of New Delhi. I have been working with Media Agencies and Production Houses like Red Chillies Entertainment, Excel Entertainment, Netflix, Ritu Kumar Label etc, capturing Behind the Scenes Film Stills, and shooting Advertising Campaings.

I also work with Models and Fashion magazines like Vogue, and photographing Celebrities for various Ad campaigns. I also specialise in Food Photography for Restaurants and Hotels.

Having started my career in the Film and Fashion Industry in Mumbai, I worked there for several years before moving to New Delhi.
